# Labor Notes Policy & Procedures
## Cortesian Bank & Reserve Policy Document

1. **Labor Notes**
  1. Labor Notes are a promissory currency managed by the CB&R for the CivMC instance of Cortesia Del Mar.
  2. Labor Notes may exist as--
    A. physical Secure Note items on CivMC, with secure ID 028065ef-13b7-4be9-b9c8-9e00880ab4e5, or
    B. promissory Secure Notes, recorded on a publicly-viewable ledger by the CB&R.
  3. 1 Labor Note must always be purchasable for 1 Player Essence.

2. **Exchange of Labor Notes**
  1. Labor Notes may be credited--
    A. by the CB&R to Cortesian nationals only, at rates intended by the CB&R, in exchange for--
      i. doing valuable work for Cortesia Del Mar, or
      ii. donating valuable resources to Cortesia Del Mar, and
    B. The method of this credit may be--
      i. via a shop chest on CivMC,
      ii. via a CB&R-approved official providing physical Labor Notes to a player on CivMC, or
      iii. crediting Labor Notes to a player's account on the promissory ledger.
  2. Labor Notes may be expended--
    A. by Cortesian nationals only, at the rates intended by the CB&R, in exchange for--
      i. resources, equipment, or services designated intentionally by the CB&R as exchangeable for Labor Notes.
    B. The method of this expense may be--
      i. via a shop chest on CivMC,
      ii. via a CB&R-approved official being given physical Labor Notes by a player on CivMC, or
      iii. subtracting Labor Notes from a player's account on the promissory ledger.
  3. A player with a positive promissory Labor Note balance may request to have some or all of their promissory balance converted into physical Labor Notes at any time. The CB&R must fulfil this request without unreasonable delay.
  4. A player with a negative promissory Labor Note balance may be required to turn in physical Labor Notes to compensate some or all of the negative promissory balance. The player must fulfil this request without unreasonable delay, unless--
    A. they do not have access to sufficient physical Labor Notes to fulfil the request, and
    B. it would be unreasonable to expect them to procure a sufficient amount within an appropriate timeframe to fulfil the original request.
  5. If an anomaly or unintended exchange arises in the Labor Note system, the CB&R reserves the right to adjust a player's Labor Note balance for the purposes of correcting the anomaly, so long as the resultant state is the product of supplementing anomalous exchanges with comparable exchanges which would have been compliant with this document and with the legitimate intentions of the affected player(s).

3. **Series Updates**
  1. At the discretion of the CB&R, Labor Notes may be updated to a new series using a new secure ID. Section 1(2)(A) of this document must be amended when this decision is implemented.
  2. When Labor Notes are updated to a new series, Labor Notes of the preceding series cease to be directly exchangeable in terms of Section (2) of this document.
  3. When Labor Notes are updated to a new series, the CB&R must permit Labor Notes of the preceding series to be exchanged for Labor Notes of the updated series at a nonzero exchange rate.

4. **Player Contract**
By engaging with the Labor Note system voluntarily, a Cortesian national agrees to the provisions of this document insofar as they apply to them or restrict their conduct.
