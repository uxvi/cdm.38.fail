Diplomatic agreements which had transient effects, but which are still part of Cortesian legal canon.

Montefiore-Shiroyama Accord
Treaty which finalized the border between Cortesia Del Mar and Nara.

Plrog Shi Interim Ownership Accord
Treaty which permitted the transfer of Plrog Shi to Cortesian sovereignty.

Second Treaty of Santiago
Treaty which effected the incorporation of Sibenica (Iaumea) into Cortesia Del Mar.
