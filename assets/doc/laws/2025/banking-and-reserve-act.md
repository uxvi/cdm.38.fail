# Banking & Reserve Act

---

1. **Title and Commencement**
  1. This act may be Cited as the “Banking & Reserve Act, 2025”
  2. This act comes into effect upon being signed by the President and Vice President.

2. **Establishment of the Cortesian Bank & Reserve (CB&R)**
  1. The CB&R is established as an auxiliary government institution.
  2. The CB&R is directed by the Treasurer, an appointed citizen who performs the operations of the CB&R on behalf of the Cortesian government.
  3. The Treasurer is appointed by the Ministry of the Interior with approval of the President or Vice President.

3. **Powers of the Cortesian Bank & Reserve**
  1. The CB&R has the sole authority over national currency balances, resource stockpiles, and any city balances and stockpiles directly controlled by the government.
  2. Permissions to withdraw from these balances are dispatched by the Treasurer.
  3. The CB&R may accrue debt on behalf of the nation, and as such issue bonds, loans, & grants to domestic or international individuals and entities at its discretion. The Treasurer must set interest rates, fees, and terms for these items.

5. **Responsibilities of the Cortesian Bank & Reserve**
  1. The CB&R must adhere to the current national budget in regards to expenditures and maintaining reserve capital to within a reasonable margin of error.
  2. The CB&R must keep a ledger of all large currency transactions, and of all bonds, loans, & grants.
  3. The CB&R must keep a ledger of all past and currently registered individuals & organizations with a brief assessment of their credit history.

6. **Powers of the Forum**
  1. The Forum may request an economic report from the Treasurer. This should at minimum consist of reserve figures & strategy, loan portfolio and projected growth, and current interest rates.
  2. The Forum may request an audit of the current budget from the Treasurer. This should consist of the appropriate records from the ledger and any erroneous transactions that occurred since the last audit was requested.
  3. These reports may be requested no earlier than 90 days apart, with at least two weeks notice to the treasurer. A vote does not need to be held to request either report.

7. **Responsibilities of the President & Vice President**
  1. The President or Vice President must draft a budget for projects, routine expenditures, and designate reserves for the balance and resource stockpile.
  2. This budget may be amended at the discretion of the President or Vice President

8. **Organizations**
  1. Businesses, Institutions, Governments, and Juristic Entities are defined as _Organizations_ under this document. If no legal definition for Juristic Entities is active, the following may be used:
    A. An organization is a group consisting of one or more individuals who are being considered as one aggregate balance and risk factor.
