# Protected Trade Act, 2025

## Title and Commencement

  1. This act may be Cited as the “Protected Trade Act, 2025”
  2. This act comes into effect upon being signed by the President and Vice President.

## Establishment of Trade Controls

1. The Interior Minister is required to create and maintain a list of items & services that are to be barred or restricted from unauthorized exchange to an international party.
2. Entries to this list may be added or removed by the National Forum by majority vote, or by the cabinet with a minimum of two weeks disclosure to the Forum.
    a. If a vote regarding a good or service is ongoing within the Forum, a hold of is to be automatically placed on the exchange of that item until the conclusion of the vote.
3. An entity who violates trade controls is to be charged with smuggling by the state.
4. If a seller wishes to distribute an item or service designated under the trade control list, they may request approval from the Treasurer for individual authorization of the sale.
   a. If the treasurer provides authorization of a sale, it must be disclosed to the Forum within one hour of the authorization.
