# Accessible Documents Resolution

## Purpose

This document repeals and replaces the *Editable Formats Resolution*.

## Implementation

1. All documents published by the forum and judicial must provide a trivially editable version in place of or along side any non-editable version.
   (1) This applies to but is not limited to: resolutions, acts, case texts, bills, and constitutional amendments.
   (2) Portable Document Format (pdf) is specifically not an editable format, unless accompanied by an editable postscript template.

2. All aforementioned documents must have their acessible copy placed into a central repository designated by the forum.
   (1) This repository may be any publicly accessible networked location.
